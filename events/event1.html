ROUND 1: Kick-Off Quiz

Goal: Warm-up quiz testing basic football knowledge.

Format: There will be 30 questions for all teams, covering topics like World Cup history, popular clubs, football legends, rules, and more.

Scoring:
• 20 questions worth 1 point each — +1 for each correct answer.
• 5 bonus questions worth 2 points each — bonus round at the end.

ROUND 2: Transfer Market Madness

Goal: Teams build their dream squad under given constraints.

Format:
• Each team gets a fictional budget (e.g., $200M).
• A list of players will be provided (or via flashcards with hidden values/positions).
• Each team must pick at least 11 players (1 GK, 4 Def, 3 Mid, 3 Fwd) and at most 15 players.
• No more than 2 players from the same club.
• Auction-style picking for maximum fun and chaos.

Scoring: Points awarded for creativity, squad balance, and meeting all conditions.
