A game focused on business planning and brand innovation, built around unique scenarios provided by our coordinators. Your creativity and entrepreneurial skills will be tested across 4 exciting stages. To become the final entrepreneur, you must successfully complete every challenge and unleash your potential!

Stage 1 – Base Scenario

  • A base scenario will be given.
  • Conducted in two rounds:
    • Round 1: Innovate a product based on the given scenario (10 minutes).
    • Round 2: Introduce the product.
  • No use of phones, laptops, or search engines.

Stage 2 – Product Development

  • Develop your product with your team members.
  • Complete all business strategies (planning, organizing, etc.).
  • Decide on:
    • Profit
    • Capital
    • Team members
    • Shares to the public
    • Product name & tagline
  • Time limit: 15 minutes.
  • No use of phones or search engines.
  • Complete the provided questionnaire (Google Form) within the allotted time.

Stage 3 – Digital Branding

  • Phones, laptops, and search engines allowed.
  • Prepare:
    • A brand logo
    • A short PPT presentation (2–3 minutes)
  • Total time: 30 minutes (including preparation and presentation).
  • Present in front of judges.
  • The best logo & PPT teams will move on to the final stage.

Stage 4 – The Final Debate

  • Only 2 teams will be selected.
  • Debate topic will be based on the given scenario.
  • Include all merits, strategies, and relevance in your arguments.
