Guidelines — Ladder to Legacy

1️⃣ Team Composition

  • Each team must consist of 4 members.
  • A maximum of 35 teams will be allowed to participate.
  • Registration will be on a first-come, first-served basis.

2️⃣ Device Policy

  • Mobile phones will be collected during the initial stages of the event to ensure fair play.
  • Phones will be returned after the completion of those rounds.

3️⃣ Code of Conduct

  • Participants must follow all instructions provided by the event coordinators.
  • Any form of cheating, misconduct, or disruption will lead to immediate disqualification.

4️⃣ Final Authority

  • The decision of the organizing committee will be final and binding in all matters related to the event.
